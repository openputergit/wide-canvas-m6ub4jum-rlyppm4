About Us

At Redleaves Tea, we invite you to embark on a journey of rich flavors, timeless traditions, and unparalleled quality. Our mission is to bring you the finest, ethically sourced teas from the best tea gardens around the globe.

Every cup is a celebration of authenticity, quality, and sustainability, ensuring you experience tea in its truest form.

Our Collection

Black Tea

Strong and full-bodied, perfect for a morning energy boost. Our black tea blends range from the malty richness of Assam to the smooth notes of Darjeeling.

Green Tea

Refreshing and packed with antioxidants, our green teas promote wellness and vitality. Try our classic Sencha or indulge in a Jasmine Green Tea.

Herbal Infusions

Naturally caffeine-free and soothing, our herbal blends are ideal for relaxation. Choose from calming chamomile, zesty lemongrass, or fruity hibiscus blends.

Oolong Tea

A perfect balance between black and green tea, offering complex flavors and a smooth finish.

Specialty Blends

For the adventurous tea lover, our specialty blends combine bold flavors like vanilla, citrus, and spices for a truly unique experience.
